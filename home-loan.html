What is a Home Loan?

A home loan is a financing facility enabling you to buy, build, or enhance a property without exhausting your savings. With a convenient payment period and affordable interest rates, it makes owning a home or enhancing the one you live in very easy.

Whether you are purchasing your dream house or want to extend the existing one, a home loan provides disciplined help. Every step is designed to keep your finances in equilibrium while allowing you to achieve long-term housing goals.

Buy Your Home | Renovate Comfortably | Manage Payments

Why Do You Need a Home Loan?

You can invest in real estate with a home loan without affecting your daily cash flow. It enables you to purchase or renovate your house while keeping other financial obligations in line. You also have the advantage of repayment terms that suit your lifestyle and income.

Structured repayment plans provide you with certainty and transparency, allowing you to reduce concerns and focus on living your life. CredBazar offers loans ranging from 20 lakhs to 50 crores, thereby allowing it to be flexible for various housing needs.

Eligibility Criteria

When applying, check the following home loan eligibility:

Criteria Requirements
Age Any person applying must be between 21 to 65 years.
Credit Score A minimum credit score of 700 or above increases approval chances.
Nationality Should be an Indian citizen.
Income Proof Clear income statements or salary certificates to establish repayment ability.
Property Plan Details of the property to purchase, build, or redevelop.
Income A monthly stable income of at least Rs 30,000 is required to get qualified for a loan.

Documents Required

Ideally, have the following documents handy to make your home loan process easy:

  • PAN & Aadhaar copies
  • Latest Utility bill
  • Salary Slips of recent 6 months
  • Bank statements 12 months
  • ITR last 2 years
  • Co-applicant KYC documents
  • Existing loan statements
  • Employment certificate proof

Home Loan Interest Rates & Charges

Home loan interest rates range from 7% to 10% depending on the applicant profile, loan amount, and category of property. All processing fees, pre-payment charges, or other fees, if charged, are clearly disclosed before granting approval.
